Security Policy

Platinum Microfinance Bank Limited - Security & Vulnerability Disclosure

Security Overview

At Platinum Microfinance Bank, we take security seriously. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of security for our digital platforms and customer data.

This security policy outlines how we handle security vulnerabilities and provides guidelines for responsible disclosure.

Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

Contact Information

Email: security@platinummfb.com

Response Time: We aim to acknowledge within 24 hours

What to Include in Your Report:
  • Description of the vulnerability
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Potential impact assessment
  • Any suggested fixes or mitigations
  • Your contact information (optional)

Responsible Disclosure Guidelines

Please Do:
  • Report vulnerabilities responsibly and privately
  • Allow reasonable time for us to address the issue
  • Act in good faith to avoid privacy violations and service disruption
  • Provide clear, detailed reports
  • Respect our customers' privacy and data
Please Don't:
  • Publicly disclose vulnerabilities before we have a chance to fix them
  • Access, modify, or destroy data that doesn't belong to you
  • Disrupt our services or systems
  • Use social engineering, phishing, or physical attacks
  • Violate any laws or regulations

Scope of Security Policy

In Scope:
  • platinum-mfb.netlify.app (main website)
  • platinum-mfb.onrender.com (API services)
  • All related subdomains and services
  • Customer-facing applications and APIs
  • Admin portals and management systems
Out of Scope:
  • Social engineering attacks
  • Physical security issues
  • Issues requiring physical access to our premises
  • Third-party services we don't control
  • Denial of service attacks

Recognition

We appreciate security researchers who help us improve our security posture through responsible disclosure. While we don't currently operate a formal bug bounty program, we may recognize researchers who provide valuable security insights.

Recognition may include acknowledgment in our security advisories or other appropriate recognition as agreed upon.
