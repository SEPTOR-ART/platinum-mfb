Our Story

Founded in 2010, Platinum Microfinance Bank was established with a vision to empower individuals and businesses through accessible financial services. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted institution, serving thousands of customers with innovative banking solutions.

Our commitment to transparency, security, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do. From savings accounts to business loans, we’re here to help you achieve your financial goals.
