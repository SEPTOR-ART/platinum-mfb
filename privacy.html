Our Privacy Commitment

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, and address when you apply for accounts, loans, or contact us.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process applications, provide services, and improve our offerings. We may also use it for marketing with your consent.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and remember your preferences. You can control cookie settings through our Cookie Policy.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The last update was July 25, 2025. Check this page for the latest version.

For questions, please contact us.
