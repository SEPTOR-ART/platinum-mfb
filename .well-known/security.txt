# Security Policy for Platinum Microfinance Bank

## Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

We take security seriously at Platinum Microfinance Bank. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

### How to Report

1. **Email**: Send details to security@platinummfb.com
2. **Do NOT** publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had a chance to address it
3. Include as much detail as possible about the vulnerability
4. Provide steps to reproduce if applicable

### What to Include

- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Any suggested fixes
- Your contact information (optional)

### Response Time

We aim to:
- Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- Provide initial assessment within 72 hours
- Keep you updated on our progress

### Scope

This security policy covers:
- platinum-mfb.netlify.app
- platinum-mfb.onrender.com
- All related subdomains and services

### Out of Scope

- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security issues
- Issues requiring physical access to our premises

### Recognition

We appreciate responsible disclosure and may recognize security researchers who help us improve our security posture.

Thank you for helping keep our customers and systems secure.
