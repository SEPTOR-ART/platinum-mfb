Platinum Microfinance Bank - Cookie Preferences & Privacy

About Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience and allow us to analyze how our website is used.

At Platinum Microfinance Bank, we are committed to protecting your privacy and giving you control over how cookies are used on our website.

Cookie Preferences

Choose which types of cookies you want to allow:

Essential Cookies
Required for basic website functionality

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They include authentication cookies, security cookies, and basic functionality cookies.

Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how you use our website

These cookies collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often. This helps us improve our website performance.

Functional Cookies
Remember your preferences and settings

These cookies remember your preferences, such as language settings and form data, to provide you with a more personalized experience.

Marketing Cookies
Used for targeted advertising and marketing

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns. Currently not used on our website.

Cookie Details

Essential Cookies:
  • session_id - Maintains your login session (expires when browser closes)
  • csrf_token - Security token for form submissions (expires when browser closes)
  • remember_token - Remembers your login for 30 days (if you choose "Remember Me")
Analytics Cookies:
  • _ga - Google Analytics tracking (expires in 2 years)
  • _gid - Google Analytics session tracking (expires in 24 hours)
  • visit_count - Tracks website visits (expires in 1 year)
Functional Cookies:
  • language - Remembers your language preference (expires in 1 year)
  • theme - Remembers your display theme (expires in 1 year)
  • form_data - Saves form data temporarily (expires in 1 hour)
